---
title: Connect Email
description: Connect your email account to Pinchy to let agents read, search, and send email on your behalf.
---

Pinchy supports two email providers. Choose your provider to get started:

- **[Google (Gmail)](/guides/connect-email-google/)** — Connect a Google Workspace or personal Gmail account using OAuth.
- **[Microsoft (Outlook / Microsoft 365)](/guides/connect-email-microsoft/)** — Connect a Microsoft 365 work or school account using OAuth.

Both providers give Pinchy permission to read, search, and send email on behalf of the connected account.
